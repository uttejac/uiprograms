Popular Places

Tirumala

Tirumala is a census town in Chittoor district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. The town is a part of Tirupati Urban Development Authority and located in Tirupati mandal of Tirupati revenue division. It is a hill town where Tirumala Venkateswara Temple is located

Sriklahasti

Srikalahasti is a holy town in Chittoor district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is a municipality and the mandal headquarters of Srikalahasti mandal in Tirupati revenue division. which is located on the banks of the River Swarnamukhi.located in chittoor dt AP

S V National park

Valleys,Waterfalls,Vegetation,Wildlife and good landscape;all add to the beauty of National Park. Named after Lord Venkateswara of Tirumala,declared a national park in 1998. The park covers an area of 354 sqkm and is home to many endangered species

Kapila Teertham

Sri Kapileswara Swamy Temple is the only temple dedicated to Lord Siva in Tirupati. It is situated about 4 km to the north of Tirupati, at the foot of the Tirumala Hills. The sacred waterfall called Kapila Teertham (also known as Alwar Teertham).

Chadragiri Fort

Chandragiri is situated in Chittoor District of AP, was the last capital of the Vijayanagara Kings. Vijayanagara Empire contributed vastly to the enrichment of Telugu culture, art & literature.The fort is 20 km from the temple town of Tirupati

S V University

Sri Venkateswara University is a state university in Tirupati,Andhra Pradesh, India.It was founded in 1954 by the then Chief minister Tanguturi Prakasam Pantulu.The university is named after Lord Venkateswara which is located in the city
